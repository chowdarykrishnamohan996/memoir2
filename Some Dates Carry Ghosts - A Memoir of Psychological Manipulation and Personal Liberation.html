About the Author

Author Photo

Chowdary Krishna Mohan

A survivor of sophisticated emotional manipulation who transformed personal trauma into psychological wisdom. Through careful analysis of lived experience, the author provides insights into recognizing and escaping covert abuse patterns. This memoir represents both personal healing journey and educational resource for others facing similar challenges.

The Journey

What started as a personal quest for understanding became a mission to help others recognize the subtle patterns of emotional manipulation. This book represents years of reflection, research, and healing, offering both vulnerability and analytical insight.
